Web development is the art of creating and maintaining websites and web applications. It encompasses a wide range of skills and technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various back-end programming languages and databases. Web developers design and build the user interfaces that we interact with daily on the internet, ensuring functionality, responsiveness, and user-friendliness. They play a crucial role in shaping the digital landscape, from simple personal blogs to complex e-commerce platforms and social media sites. With the rapid evolution of technology, web development is an ever-changing field, requiring continuous learning and adaptation to stay relevant in the digital age.
