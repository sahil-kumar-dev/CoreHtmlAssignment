Web design is the creative process of crafting visually appealing and user-friendly websites. It involves a combination of graphic design, layout planning, typography, and color theory to create a seamless and engaging user experience. Web designers focus on both the aesthetics and functionality of a site, ensuring it not only looks visually appealing but also functions smoothly across various devices and browsers. They strive to communicate a brand's message effectively and intuitively through the design elements. With the ever-evolving digital landscape, web design remains vital in capturing and retaining user attention, making it an integral part of the online world's success.
